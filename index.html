About Us

Adarsha Secondary School, Lalmatiya was established in 1960 AD (2017 BS) as a community-owned academic institute located in Lalmaitya, Deukhurai, Dang district of Nepal. This school is affiliated with the (NEB) National Examinations Board and approved by Ministry of Education (MoE), Nepal. It offers Ten plus Two (10+2) programs in Management, Humanities, Science and Education streams.

Adarsha Secondary School, Lalmatiya, Dang provides the basic level to +2 (Secondary) level education with affordable fees structures. It also offers scholarships schemes for deserving, hardworking, and the socially deprived students of the society. This school has been performing excellent result on internal and final examination.
